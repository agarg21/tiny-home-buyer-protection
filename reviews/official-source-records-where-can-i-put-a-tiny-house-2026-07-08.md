## Deferred Candidate: Austin, Texas

Recommendation: `defer`

Official sources reviewed:

- `https://www.austintexas.gov/development-services/home-amendments`
- `https://www.austintexas.gov/development-services/additional-dwelling-units`
- `https://services.austintexas.gov/edims/document.cfm?id=219157`

Reason for deferral:

- Austin's current HOME Amendments page gives a current definition for tiny homes/tiny houses and describes permit application options.
- Austin's ADU page gives current ADU requirements and says structures meeting dwelling-unit criteria must follow zoning, utility meter, internal access, occupancy, and address requirements.
- The older City memo discusses tiny houses on wheels and RV-style utility hookups, but it is not enough by itself to create a current placement path for THOWs.
- A publishable record needs another pass through the current Land Development Code, forms, and Residential Plan Review requirements to separate:
  - IRC tiny home / tiny house as a dwelling unit.
  - ADU / additional dwelling unit path.
  - RV or THOW path.
  - Short-term rental and certificate-of-occupancy constraints.

Recommended next step:

- Research Austin as a second-wave record after Los Angeles, San Diego, and Georgia DCA are implemented.
