Tiny Homes Buyer Protection

Make tiny-home decisions with fewer surprises.

A practical planning site for people checking zoning, land, cost, utilities, financing, insurance, and builder trust before they buy or build a tiny home.

First Tool In Progress

Where Can I Put a Tiny House?

The MVP will be a placement triage tool that distinguishes THOWs, park models, ADUs, RVs, modular homes, manufactured homes, foundation tiny homes, and small cabins.

It will not give definitive legal answers. It will show possible placement paths, official-source checks, and local verification steps.

Publishing Standard

  • Use official sources for zoning, codes, permits, health, and utility claims.
  • Separate measured facts, estimates, assumptions, and opinions.
  • Avoid generic inspiration content and thin affiliate lists.
  • Prefer tools, calculators, checklists, tables, and source-backed guides.
