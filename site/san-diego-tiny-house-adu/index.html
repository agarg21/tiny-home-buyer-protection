Official sources

What the official sources say

Movable tiny house bulletin

Information Bulletin 403 is the city's movable tiny house-specific official bulletin and should be used for implementation details, with current code verified before publishing a high-specificity claim.

City of San Diego Information Bulletin 403

ADU and JADU code section

San Diego Municipal Code Section 141.0302 governs ADUs and JADUs and contains restrictions on rental term, lot/zone context, size, setbacks, coastal overlay, fire, parking, owner occupancy, and related requirements.

San Diego Municipal Code Chapter 14, Article 1, Division 3

Adopted code update context

City of San Diego official materials describe movable tiny houses as adopted into companion unit / junior unit regulations and as falling under the state's ADU definition.

Before you buy

Verification checklist

Use this as a question list before buying land, ordering a movable tiny house, or making a nonrefundable deposit.

  1. Confirm the parcel is inside the City of San Diego, not merely San Diego County.
  2. Ask whether the parcel, zone, and existing/proposed dwelling context fit the current ADU / companion unit-related framework.
  3. Ask how coastal overlay, fire hazard severity, beach impact parking, access, and utility requirements affect the site.
  4. Ask whether the specific movable tiny house design meets current city standards.
  5. Confirm rental-term, parking, owner-occupancy, address, utility, lender, and insurer constraints.
  6. Save official URLs, staff names, dates, and written answers in a source log.
