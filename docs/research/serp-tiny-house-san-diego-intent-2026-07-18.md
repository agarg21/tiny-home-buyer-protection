## Intent Read

Primary likely intents:

- Buy or browse tiny homes in/near San Diego.
- Find San Diego-area tiny home builders.
- Find tiny house communities or examples near San Diego.
- Explore short-stay/vacation tiny houses.
- Ask general local feasibility questions.

Secondary intent:

- Understand whether tiny homes on wheels are allowed in San Diego or San Diego
  County.

This is not a high-confidence placement-only query. A pure zoning/source page
would be intent-mismatched unless it is framed as a buyer-protective companion
to shopping/building intent.

## Strategy Implications

Do not target `tiny house san diego` with only a cautious zoning memo.

The better angle is an answer-first San Diego buyer page that acknowledges the
mixed intent:

- Are you trying to buy/build, stay, join a community, or place one legally?
- What the official San Diego city source supports.
- What changed or differs for unincorporated San Diego County.
- Which searches are probably shopping/stay intent versus placement intent.
- What to ask before contacting a builder, seller, community, or Airbnb-style
  host.

The current San Diego page can still support this query, but only if it becomes
more helpful above the fold. It should not pretend the query is just
`tiny house zoning san diego`.
